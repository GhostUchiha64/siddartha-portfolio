About Me

I am a Data Science graduate student at the University of North Dakota with a strong foundation in machine learning, statistical analysis, and data visualization. My experience spans financial data analysis, business intelligence, and predictive modeling, where I apply data-driven techniques to extract actionable insights and support decision-making.

Previously, I worked as a Data Analyst Intern at Mashreq Bank, analyzing complex financial datasets and building predictive models. I also contributed as a Runway Assistant at the Center for Innovation, assisting entrepreneurs in refining business ideas and strategy development. Additionally, I have hands-on experience in bibliometric research analysis, leveraging machine learning to study research team composition and funding impact.

I am proficient in Python, R, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, and cloud computing technologies, with expertise in data processing, visualization, and AI-driven solutions. Passionate about using data science to solve real-world challenges, I am always eager to learn, adapt, and innovate.
